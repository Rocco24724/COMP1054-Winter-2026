About Tiger Jaw's hit song "Ghost"

The song "Ghost" is the seventh track on Tiger Jaw's upcoming album "Lost on You" planned for release on March 27, 2026. According to Tiger Jaw the song "Ghost" was inspired by their ability to turn personal experiences into a widely understood sentiment. Ben Walsh of Tiger Jaw explains, “The genesis of "Ghost" was a chance run-in with a person I grew up with. A run-in that would have been warm, welcomed and cherished at one point in my life held no significance after the slow steady passage of time and growing apart. The quick flash of memories with this person felt like a glimpse into a past life, or like seeing a ghost. Some people don't stay in your life forever and that's OK, but it's very interesting which memories get dredged up when you see a once familiar face.”

About Tiger Jaw

Tigers Jaw is an indie rock band from Scranton, Pennsylvania, formed in 2005 by Adam McIlwee and Ben Walsh, with Walsh remaining the group’s constant member over the years. Known for blending elements of indie rock, Midwest emo, and pop punk, the band has evolved through several lineup changes and periods of uncertainty while continuing to release well‑received albums, including Charmer (2014), Spin (2017), and I Won’t Care How You Remember Me (2021). Their sound has been described as emotionally driven, introspective, and rooted in both classic and modern indie sensibilities, with influences ranging from Fall Out Boy and My Chemical Romance to Fleetwood Mac.
